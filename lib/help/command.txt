--- Melee attack ---

If you walk toward a monster, you can attack it by your weapon.

--- Running ---

If you input shift + move keys, you can run.

--- Staying with attack ---

Control + move keys.

--- Repeating ---

Push '0' and input a number of repeating. Then push a command key.
e.g. 099s -> Searches during 99 turns.

--- Macro ---

Use '@' command to set Macro commands.
