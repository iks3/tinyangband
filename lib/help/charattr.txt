=== Character Attributes ===

--- stats ---

6 stats are important.

Str: Affects blows and damages of melee attacks, and weight you can carry.
Int: Affects MP and ability to cast for Sorcery, and using magic devices.
Wis: Affects MP and ability of cast for Life, and magical deffence.
Dex: Affects number of blows and accuracy of melee attacks, and AC.
Con: Affects your HP.
Chr: Shopping.

--- Experience ---

Your character levels up when they gets enough EXP.
They'll gain more HP and MP, increase one stat, and receive a reward from 
their patron.

--- Money (AU) ---

Value of your gold and other precious metals and jewels.

--- AC (Armor Class) ---

Affects both ability to avoid and defend against blows.  When high you are
well protected from melee attacks.

--- HP (Hit Point) ---

When HP become less than 0, you die.

--- SP (Spell Point) ---

Magic power.  You need it to cast spells.

--- Other attributes ---

There are various other attributes, particularly of note are ...

Stealth: When high monsters are slow to wake up.
Speed: When high you can move faster than the enemy.
