=== Attacking ===

--- Melee attack ---

If you walk toward a monster, you can attack it by your weapon.
If your to-hit bonus is larger, you can hit it more.
If its AC is larger, it can avoid your attacks easier.

Weapons that are heavy and have large dice is good for melee attacks.

--- Shooting ---

When you equip a sling, a bow or a crossbow, you can fire a shot an 
arrow or a bolt.

--- Spell casting ---

If you cast attack spells, you can attack monsters by spells.
There are bolts, beams, and balls in attack spells.

You can attack monsters by magic devices as same as attack spells.
